About BellaNotte 🇮🇹

BellaNotte is a traditional Italian restaurant serving authentic dishes. The difference is that we use smart tables and artificial intelligence systems so customers can personalize their experience, order comfortably, and enjoy faster and more interactive service. 🇮🇹✨

Menu Highlights 🍕

🍕 Pizza Margherita

A classic pizza with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and basil. At BellaNotte, AI controls the oven temperature and exact baking time so the crust is perfectly crispy and the cheese melts just right.

🍝 Spaghetti alla Bolognese

Italian pasta with homemade Bolognese sauce. AI calculates the exact cooking time for the pasta and adjusts the sauce to ensure perfect texture and taste every time.

🍰 Tiramisù

Traditional Italian dessert with layers of mascarpone cream and coffee-soaked sponge cake. Thanks to AI, portions are served perfectly even and fresh, keeping the ideal temperature until it reaches your table.

☕ Caffè Italiano

An intense, aromatic espresso. At BellaNotte, AI programs the coffee machine to achieve the perfect pressure and temperature, guaranteeing the same authentic flavor every time.

🍋 Limoncello

A typical Italian liqueur made from fresh lemons. At BellaNotte, AI keeps the bottle at the perfect temperature and serves the exact amount for a refreshing taste every time.

AI Features 🤖

  • Smart tables with touch screens for choosing side dishes, ordering, or calling the waiter.
  • Hidden compartments in the table open automatically to offer salt, pepper, olive oil, or Parmesan cheese when selected on the screen.
  • Personalized recommendations based on your preferences or allergies.
  • Italian music that changes according to the restaurant atmosphere.
  • Fast and secure payments directly from the table.

Customer Experience 🎶

  • Customers see their name on the table screen when they arrive with a reservation.
  • Use the touchscreen to browse the menu and choose side dishes.
  • Receive condiments directly from the table without waiting.
  • Call the waiter or request the bill easily from the screen.
  • Enjoy Italian music and beautiful landscapes displayed on the table screens while waiting for food.
  • Pay quickly without waiting for a waiter to bring the bill.

Why Visit BellaNotte? 🏛️

BellaNotte combines authentic Italian food with modern technology. Smart tables make the experience fun and comfortable. Everything is faster and more personalized, making you feel like you're dining in Italy without leaving your city. 🇮🇹
